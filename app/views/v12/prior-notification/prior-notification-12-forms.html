How to action patients because of mental capacity or patient choice?

You will need to:

  • download the correct form
  • print the form
  • get the patient to sign the form (Informed patients choice only)
  • a GP, nurse or clinician will need to fill in the form and sign it
  • scan the form
  • submit the form to the CSAS website (opens in new window)
