You should only create a dummy registration as a last resort if a participant cannot be matched in the system

  • you can send this result to the patient
  • they will be invited for screening based on their recall status
  • you can view their patient summary on the system
  • you can add future test results to their record and view their screening history
  • you can cease and defer them from screening
