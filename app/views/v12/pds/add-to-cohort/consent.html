Gaining patient consent

New patients must give permission to be added to the screening programme.

For consent to be valid, it must be voluntary and informed, and the patient consenting must have the capacity to make the decision.

Consent can be given:

  • verbally – for example, saying they're happy to be invited for screening
  • in writing – for example, signing a consent form
